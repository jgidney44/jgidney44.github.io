<p>I am a first-year PhD student in Pure Mathematics at the University of Birmingham under the supervision of <a href="https://harper-scott.github.io/">Dr Scott Harper</a>. My research lies in finite group theory and representation theory,
with a focus on the subgroup structure of the finite classical groups. I am currently working on bounds for the sizes of normal coverings of these groups.</p>

<p>I completed a Master's in Mathematics at the University of Bristol in 2025. My dissertation, supervised by <a href="https://seis.bristol.ac.uk/~tb13602/index.html">Professor Tim Burness</a>, focused on the degree of commutativity of finite groups — the probability that two
randomly chosen elements of a group commute.</p>
