I am a PhD Pure Mathematics student at the University of Birmingham under the supervision of Dr Scott Harper. My research interests lie in finite group theory and representation theory.
I am currently investigating bounds on the sizes of normal coverings of the classical groups, expanding on work of Britnell and Maroti in 2014. In 2025, I completed a Master's in Mathematics at the University of 
Bristol. My dissertation was on the degree of commutativity of finite groups, the probability that two randomly chosen elements from a group commute.
