I am a PhD Pure Mathematics student at the University of Birmingham under the supervision of Dr Scott Harper. My research lies in finite group theory and representation theory,
in particular the subgroup structure of the finite classical groups. I am currently working on bounds for the sizes of normal coverings of the finite classical groups, 
expanding on work of Britnell and Maroti in 2014. 
<br>
I completed a Master's in Mathematics at the University of Bristol in 2025. My dissertation focused on the degree of commutativity of finite groups 
- the probability that two randomly chosen elements from a group commute.
