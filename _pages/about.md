<p>I am a PhD student in Pure Mathematics at the University of Birmingham under the supervision of Dr Scott Harper. My research lies in finite group theory and representation theory,
with a focus on the subgroup structure of the finite classical groups. I am currently working on bounds for the sizes of normal coverings of these groups, 
expanding on work of Britnell and Mar&oacute;ti in 2014.</p>

<p>I completed a Master's in Mathematics at the University of Bristol in 2025. My dissertation, supervised by Professor Tim Burness, focused on the degree of commutativity of finite groups - the probability that two
randomly chosen elements of a group commute.</p>
